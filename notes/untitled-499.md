> **When you pursue an opportunity based on delivering quantifiable business value to the client and to IBM and focus on that during every sales stage** > ***from qualification to closing the deal,*** > **you invest IBM resources in opportunities IBM is likely to win.

The IBM Client Engagement Model (The Model) practice Pursue Based on Value provides you with an** > ***evidence-based approach*** > **to evaluating whether to invest your time and resources to pursue an opportunity or whether to disengage. For opportunities you effectively qualify** > ***and*** > **choose to pursue, the Pursue Based on Value approach provides you guidance to create and capture compelling value for the client through the Model sales stages to close the deal.
The Pursue Based on Value approach is operationalized in**> **[ IBM Sales Cloud (ISC)](https://ibmsc.lightning.force.com/lightning/page/home)** > **through the Qualifier and Pursuit Profiler capabilities. Note: You will need an ISC license to access the Qualifier and the Pursuit Profiler capabilities.
