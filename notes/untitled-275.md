*I told you before. I already went back and stopped you from making that mistake, but then you didn't learn from it and made a worse mistake. Then you asked me to go and fix the original mistake so you would learn from it. I know you don't remember all this, so I'll just say, "no."*

Fun aside, we all have difficult past situations, and if you're alive to post about it in a TT sub, things can't be all that bad. Speaking from the future of my past, I'd tell myself changing those things I wanted to change wouldn't have had good outcomes: I'd be in jail, homeless, or dead. If you're not any of those, then you might be living the best timeline for you.
