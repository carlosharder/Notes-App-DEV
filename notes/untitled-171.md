Thank you for registering for the upcoming US National Sales Acceleration Workshop being held Aug 14-15 in Chicago.
In a hands-on environment with your US National colleagues, you will learn new OI and progression techniques and work on your own active opportunities and territories with sales coaches. 

To maximize this unique learning opportunity, the following is important **required pre-work**:
# Select one of your opportunities for your deal progression focus.   
	* Complete the [Relationship Barometer](https://ibm.seismic.com/app#/doccenter/5477419a-9474-4c51-94af-b442e9169fab/doc/%252Fdd98c5a3df-6b7c-1d77-6f07-d12e63954c78%252FdfOTRiYmU4NTQtNWY4NC03Y2QyLWZjYWUtOGIxYmFmZjkyZThk%252CPT0%253D%252CSG93LXRv%252Flfa21ecff2-3ead-472e-bdae-cf0ccfbb9b36//?mode=view&parentPath=sessionStorage&anchorId=d2977e51-b9fc-442e-bb8d-00ff4b6d46e8) and [Pursuit Profiler](https://ibm.seismic.com/Link/Content/DCDBXDX7663Dc8cPC2TfJPQDWc93) for your chosen opportunity. This will help you to evaluate your opportunity’s strengths and vulnerabilities as well as client relationships.

	* Complete the *Pursue Based on Value* Sales Accelerator [here.](https://yourlearning.ibm.com/activity/ILB-YZMRWZGNKXGMK9G4)

# Ensure you have access to LinkedIn Sales Navigator and ZoomInfo.
	* **If you require LinkedIn Sales Navigator access, please follow the instructions** **[here.](https://ibm.ent.box.com/notes/955905156144?s=wvo7ykjtkdr66cnfh25qxlr424ka26gk)****If you require ZoomInfo access, please contact** **[Taylor Joniak](mailto:taylor@ibm.com)****.****Ensure you have a list of your accounts in CSV format.** 




**Other important notes**:
* Both days are mandatory, and you are expected to fully participate (see agenda at-a-glance below).
* We will be in room 6607/6609 (IBM Chicago office @ 71 S. Wacker) for the majority of our time together. Breakout exercises on Day 1 will be conducted in breakout rooms throughout the IBM Chicago office.
* If you are travelling into Chicago, it is assumed that you will align your travel with client and/or partner meetings that have been arranged or are being arranged.

* Morning coffee and lunch will be provided each day.
Wednesday, August 14 – Pipeline Progression (8:30AM – 5:30PM CT)
* Compelling Reasons to Act (CRA) – based on your selected opportunity
	* Content re: Why? Why IBM? Why Now?
	* Hands-on: Participants iteratively develop their CRA
	* Breakout Exercise – CRA with sales manager coaches
	* In groups of approx 5, each seller will review their CRA, get coaching, and deliver a 90 second elevator pitch


* One Page Value Propositions – based on your selected opportunity
	* Content, best practices and examples related to how best to construct and deliver the One Page Value Prop.
	* Hands-on: Participants develop a first draft of their One Page Value Proposition
	* Breakout Exercise – One Page Value with sales manager coaches
	* In groups of approx 5, each seller conducts a 12-minute role play with their coach (client) in client situation context.


* BP Presentation from DataHub
Thursday, August 15 – Prospect With Impact (8:30AM – 4:30PM CT)
* Best practices OI instruction and hands-on activities (collaboration and feedback in small working groups throughout the day)
* Hands-on use of LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Zoominfo, ISC Client Interests, Goldmine 2.0, and Salesloft within the context of the OI process:
	* Account Prioritization & Research 
	* Lead Identification Research
	* Effective Messaging Techniques
	* Cadencing with Salesloft + Tracking Outreach with LISN/ISC Integration
