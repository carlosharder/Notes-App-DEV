Ongoing Support and Best Wishes for the New Year
 
Dear Carlos and Esther,
 
I hope this email finds you well. First, **I would like to thank you for your work and support for Building New Hope (BNH)**. This time of the year all nonprofits are trying to reach their donors to raise funds, and for us it is not different. As you may know, **the end-of-year appeal holds immense significance for BNH, especially in sustaining our operations**.

Our work in Nicaragua thrives due to the generosity of donors and volunteers like yourself, and your insider perspective makes your support even more meaningful. 
 
This year Diana, Deyfitz and Armando graduated, marking significant milestones in their lives. Witnessing their achievements reaffirms the invaluable and necessary impart of the work we are doing. **Scholarships and our funds for education are indispensable for the success of the kids.** It is a testament to the effectiveness of our scholarship programs and the dedicated funds allocated for education. These initiatives are not just investments; they are lifelines that empower young individuals to overcome challenges and reach new heights. The scholarships and educational support we provide are vital components that ensure the success and well-being of these remarkable individuals.
 
But our work doesn’t stop there. **This year we are again buying 15,000 pounds of green beans at $2.80 the pound, this is thanks to all coffee buyers.** Additionally, **we are committed to supporting our partners at El Porvenir by re-establishing a nursery for coffee seedlings**. This nursery is crucial for their ability to grow their own coffee plants, ensuring long-term sustainability.
 
**Your support has been instrumental in reaching this point, and I am hopeful that you will consider extending your help once again.**
Every contribution, regardless of size, plays a vital role in ensuring the success and sustainability of our mission. If currently you find it challenging to donate, we completely understand. However, we would appreciate your support in a different way, by either spreading the word about our mission, buying coffee, or sharing this email with friends and family. Your voice can make a significant impact in raising awareness and reaching those who might be able to contribute.
 
**To contribute please visit** [https://www.buildingnewhope.org/donate](https://www.buildingnewhope.org/donate) or you can send a check payable to Building New Hope and send it to 6401 Penn Ave, 3rd Floor, Pittsburgh PA 15206.
 
**As we step into the New Year, I extend my warmest wishes to you.** May the coming year be filled with joy, prosperity, and new opportunities.  Thanks once again for being a vital part of our journey. Happy New Year!!!!
 
Warm regards,
 
Soledad
