## **Executive Summary**
Blue Shield of California wants to fully migrate to the Cloud by 2026. One of the leaders of these efforts is Mike Peterson, VP of Infrastructure. Mike comes from Expedia, an important client of Turbonomic. Blue Shield’s migration to the cloud started in 2022, the year Mike Peterson moved from Expedia to Blue Shield California. While he does not have healthcare industry experience, he comes with the success badge of reducing the direct infrastructure expenditure of Expedia by $400M per year. 
He intends to focus on Cloud Migration and other project that will increase the operational stability of Blue Shield. He also wants to re-shape the client experience so that it is more retail-like. He stated that wants to create an “instant gratification” level of interaction between BlueShield health care platforms and the clients. To do so, he must focus to the Blue Shield’s mobile applications. And he will need a very responsive infrastructure of the likes of Expedia and other client-centered organizations. 
There are 3 AIOps that can help. 
* Turbonomic
* Instana
* InstaBug
* And possibly….SevOne
