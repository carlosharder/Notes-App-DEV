Week 0
* Create the VM where the appliance will live during the PoC.
* Secure credentials (read-only) of the what Turbonomic will map
* pre-Download the ova (it can take more than on hour)

Week 1
- Meeting to install the OVA and 
- In a Webex meeting we walk through there process of installation
- If the OVA is already pre-downloaded, it can take less than 45 minutes. 
- Typically on this first installation meeting we connect Turbonomic with the Vcenter and one or two other targets, such as storage or an application monitoring system. 
Week 2 
- If possible we add one ore more sources of information (targets) to Turbonomic
- We start demonstrating the platform using the client’s own data
- The client has full access to the platform during the complete evaluation time, not only during the 1 hour meetings with IBM. 
Week 3 - 4  
- Depending on the client’s level of interest, we often shift to 2 meetings per week
- During this time we continue demonstrating the platform 
- In some case the client will chose to allow the demonstration of real-time automations. 
- In collaboration with the client, we start preparing an analysis of the value created by Turbonomic. 
- The executive team is invited to review preliminary results
Week 5 - 6  
- Many client’s at this point do request an extension of the trial licenses. It is possible to extend the engagement a couple week to allow for a greater understanding of the potential use cases. 
- By week 5 a 1st draft report is completed and presented to the team.
