They have engaged IBM to assess Turbo's capabilities and are nearing completion of a PoC using temp licenses (extended twice and due to expire at the end of July). 
Environment/endpoints to be managed are approximately 20k.
Business Challenge:
1) Hundreds of production and non-production servers currently run at 50% utilization. Improve utilization and reduce infra costs
2) There are alternative resources in both data centers resulting in too much capacity through out the environments. 'Thin out' the capacity needed for duplication of env. in DR.
3) Application teams are afraid to modify their environment and cost gets passed down to infrastructure.
