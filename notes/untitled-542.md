 # What is FinOps X?
FinOps X is the home of FinOps, enabling the FinOps community to come together in person. Hosted by the [FinOps Foundation](https://www.finops.org/), providing the ultimate networking and learning opportunity for anyone working in FinOps.
COMMUNITY
There is no explaining what you do or what FinOps is at this conference. Get straight into deeper level conversations and grow your network.
BEST PRACTICES
Hear the latest on FinOps best practices and what is in the future for FinOps. Meet industry thought leaders and contribute to the conversation.
LEARNING
Learn and develop your skillsets for different approaches to FinOps challenges from a range of industry and organisational perspectives.
